FEATURES:

	Calculate commute for your specific track
	Enter your home location or auto-find optimal spot
	See hours/day, week, month, year
	Calculate gas costs & car maintenance
	See UWorld questions lost
	Month-by-month breakdown

HOW TO GET YOUR COORDINATES:

	Google Maps Method:
	1. Find your address in Google Maps
	2. Right-click on the location
	3. Click the coordinates (like "26.082, -80.249")
	4. Paste into calculator

	OR

	Leave it blank and let the tool find the optimal location
	(Takes a while, but finds the best spot automatically)
